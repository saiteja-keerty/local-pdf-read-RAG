# Insurance glossary (minimal seed)

- Deductible: The amount you pay out-of-pocket before insurance starts paying.
- Copayment (copay): A fixed amount you pay for a covered service (e.g., $20).
- Coinsurance: A percentage of costs you pay after meeting your deductible.
- Out-of-pocket maximum (OOP): The most you'll pay in a plan year; after hitting it, insurance pays 100% of covered services.
- Explanation of Benefits (EOB): A statement from the insurer explaining what was billed, what they allowed, and what you owe.
- In-network vs Out-of-network: In-network providers have negotiated rates; out-of-network often costs more.

# UnitedHealthcare-specific notes
